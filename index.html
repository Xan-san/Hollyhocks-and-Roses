About Us

We are a family run business with over fifteen years experience in the wedding and events business, based in the market town of Mold in North Wales.


We have a large selection of vintage crockery and glassware, as well as all sorts of unique items available to hire for weddings and special events.


We specialise in providing a tailored service so whether you simply want to hire a few items or would like us to source items for you, plan the whole event and set up on the day, it is totally up to you!


Our aim is to provide a quality, reliable service at reasonable prices.


Many of our items are unique, therefore early booking is essential, contact us to find out how we can help you create special memories for your special event.


Please email or telephone us to check availability for your chosen date and to discuss your requirements.
